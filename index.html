WELCOME VISITOR !


CommandoThug is a canvas based action game I made during the years 2014/2015

It's based on the 2D game framework called "Phaser" which can be found here


This game is now licensed under GPL v3

Get started !












CONTROLS

KEYBOARD


Use keyboard arrows to move your soldier

UP to jump, DOWN to crouch, LEFT and RIGHT to move

MOUSE


Use the mouse pointer to move your soldier

Your soldier will follow the mouse pointer

MOBILE


Similar to mouse controls but with touch inputs

Your soldier moves toward touch inputs

2 Players


Available in the game's options menu

Player1 with mouse
Player2 with keyboard

. . .

- - -  The soldier aims and shoots automatically at the closest target  - - -

- - -  Objective of the game is to make your team win by killing as many soldiers of the opposite team as possible  - - -
